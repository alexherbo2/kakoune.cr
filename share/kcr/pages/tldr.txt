Start a new session:
	ks kanto
	kcr shell --session kanto

Connect to the current session:
	a
	kcr attach

Connect to a session:
	K
	kcr-fzf-shell

List sessions:
	kl
	kcr list

Open a file:
	k pokemon.json
	kcr edit pokemon.json

Open files to a session:
	:n
	kcr-fzf-shell --working-directory . nnn

Print the current buffer:
	:cat
	kcr cat --raw
